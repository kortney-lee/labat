# WIHY 30-Day Follower Growth Plan

## The Promise (3-Second Rule)

> **"We scan food so you don't have to guess."**

Every post ties back to one behavior: **scan → ask → decide.**
People follow because they know exactly what they'll get — bold, fast answers about what's actually healthy.

---

## 5 Recurring Series

| # | Series | Format | Hook Pattern | Shania Template |
|---|--------|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| 1 | **Scan This** | 15–30s video + graphic | "I scanned [product] and here's what's actually in it" | `ingredient_breakdown` for graphic recap |
| 2 | **Better Swap** | 15–30s video + graphic | "[Popular item] vs [better option] — the difference is wild" | `comparison_split` for side-by-side |
| 3 | **Healthy Myth** | 15–30s video + graphic | "Everyone thinks [food] is healthy. Here's the truth." | `stat_card` or `research_card` for data |
| 4 | **What the Label Really Means** | 15–30s video + graphic | "This label says 'natural.' Here's what that actually means." | `hook_square` / `hook_vertical` for bold claim |
| 5 | **Is It Worth Eating?** | 15–30s video + graphic | "Is [food] worth eating? Let's find out." | `quote_card` for verdict, `stat_card` for data |

## Week 1 — Launch the Machine (Days 1–7)

## Rules

1. Every post answers one question. Never two.
2. The first line/frame is the hook. Spend 50% of your effort there.
3. Every graphic post has a companion story repost with a poll or question sticker.
4. Reply to every comment in the first hour.
5. Never post without a CTA — "scan it," "try the app," "save this."
6. If a post gets 2x saves vs average, make a sequel within 48 hours.
7. Video thumbnails use `hook_square` or `hook_vertical` templates for consistency.
