## What To Do Next

- **Plan Your Meals:** Pick two to three protein-rich recipes that you enjoy and can easily prepare. Try to choose meals that share common ingredients to save time and money.
  
- **Shop Smart:** Create a detailed grocery list that includes bulk items and versatile ingredients. Stick to the list to resist impulse buys.

- **Prep in Batches:** Set aside a few hours on the weekend to cook and portion out meals for the week. Use clear containers to keep track of what you have.

- **Stay Flexible:** While consistency is important, allow a bit of flexibility to use up leftovers or take advantage of sales on fresh produce.
