# Why You Overeat (And What Actually Works)

When people blame willpower, they miss the larger pattern. Behavior is shaped by environment and repetition.

## Common trigger stack

- high-friction meal prep
- low-protein first meals
- stress-heavy afternoons
- easy access to hyper-palatable snacks

## What data-driven fixes look like

1. Pre-commit one reliable meal structure.
2. Put high-satiety options at first reach.
3. Use fixed snack windows to reduce grazing.
4. Audit one trigger each week and remove friction.
