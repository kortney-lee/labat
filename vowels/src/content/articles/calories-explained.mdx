# Calories Explained Without the Confusion

Calories are a unit of energy. They are not moral labels. The reason people struggle is usually not math. It is environment, hunger, routine, and stress.

## What calories can tell you

Calories estimate energy intake and help compare options.

## What calories cannot tell you

Calories do not directly capture satiety, food quality, or habit durability.

## Practical framework

- anchor each meal with protein and fiber
- keep convenient defaults that reduce decision fatigue
- track trend direction weekly, not hourly
